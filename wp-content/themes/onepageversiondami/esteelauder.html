Estée Lauder

1 Fitzroy, London W1

Project Description
Delivering hotel quality for global consumer brand in Fitzrovia.

Estée Lauder’s new dynamic London base encourages employee collaboration at the former Middlesex Hospital site. A core business aim of the move was to bring together and raise internal brand awareness amongst the teams for over 30 brands; from established global brands as well as newer market-leading additions. Within the fresh new office space, these brands are promoted internally through dramatic graphics on “brand hubs” for each product line and the on-site employee store. For client meetings, a smart technology booking system cleverly transforms rooms with images and signage to showcase a selected brand. An awe inspiring bronze clad staircase links to the new flagship international training facility. Further smart technology supports the hotel spa, hair salons, brand gallery and a suite of hi-tech training rooms over the 2 floors that form these state of the art facilities.

One of the largest ever West End lettings, BW improved the timescales for sectional completions, delivering 3 floors early to help the clients move strategy. Daily meetings with the client stakeholders to turn the initial concept into design all the way through to completion ensured the shared philosophy of strong teamwork was fully embraced. “Real time“ working with designers based full time at the site office minimised time in accommodating these live changes. Following the initial project award of 8 floors, the client soon negotiated 2 further floors with BW to accommodate further expansion. 1 Fitzroy Place is now an inspiring, agile space that will foster a sense of community and reflect the client’s core values.

Scope and Services
  • Cat B fit out of 140,000 sq ft of office space across eight floors.
  • Dramatic 4,000 sq ft hotel style lobby, plus eight floors incorporating office space, three street level retail units, two training floors, an elite executive floor with luxurious meeting space and external terraces including dedicated space for summer meetings.
  • High end bespoke joinery throughout, state of the art integrated AV within the salons, bespoke cantilevered bronze-clad staircase.
Challenges
  • Achieving the timescales set by the project’s fast track programme, whilst delivering high quality bespoke joinery, including sourcing specialist fabricators and designing structural solutions to deliver the clients vision for the two storey bronze-clad staircase.
  • Meeting the high acoustic criteria set within the brief.
  • Creating a superior integrated AV system throughout the first and second floor training salons.
The BW Added Value
  • BW’s commitment to quality enabled us to deliver an exceptional development that met this notable client’s high expectations, whilst using design engineering options to assist with budget constraints.
  • BW successfully maintained the developments BREEAM Excellent rating and the SKA Gold rating is on target.
