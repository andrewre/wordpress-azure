General Statement Of The Health & Safety Policy

Policy

BW regard Health & Safety at Work as a business priority and require the co-operation of all employees to establish and maintain high standards. In accordance with our Health & Safety Management System, which complies with BS OHSAS 18001:2007, we believe all injuries and occupational ill health can be prevented and in pursuit of this, we give details of our policy statement and our commitment to:

  • Provide the resources necessary to create and maintain a safe and healthy working environment and systems of work for all our employees, contractors and visitors.
  • Commit to the prevention of injury and ill health and continual improvement in occupational Health & Safety management and performance.
  • Comply with all applicable legal requirements and to any other occupational Health & Safety requirements to which we subscribe.

  • Provide such information, training and supervision as is needed for this purpose.
  • Prevent our activities and omissions adversely affecting the Health & Safety of others.
  • Regularly set and review Health & Safety objectives and targets at all levels within the company. Ensure every person’s responsibility and accountability for safety is clearly defined and understood.
  • Ensure Health & Safety is effectively implemented and monitored, is kept up to date and reflects business change both internally and externally, there will be a formal annual review of the policy and the way in which it is operated.
  • Work in partnership with the workforce on matters of Health & Safety.

This policy will be made available to all employees and other interested parties. Each manager will ensure that the necessary organisation and arrangements are in place in support of this policy.

Responsibilities

The chief executive officer is ultimately responsible for health and safety.

Contracts managers are responsible for overseeing health and safety management on site and monitoring the effective implementation of all safety procedures.

Project managers are responsible for maintaining the competent supervision of subcontractors and operatives on site and implementing safety procedures.

Our Health & Safety advisors assist with the formulation, implementation and monitoring of Health & Safety procedures and systems, advising senior management on training and resource matters, investigating accidents and reporting on safety to senior management.

All employees are responsible for the safety of themselves and others who may be affected by their acts and must comply with the requirements of their company health, safety and environment handbook and staff handbook.

Company Health & Safety and Environmental Handbook

All BW employees are issued with a copy of our company Health & Safety and environmental handbook on commencement of their employment.

This handbook gives details on the company’s policies, requirements and standards of good practice and reflects the company’s commitment with regard to all Health & Safety and environmental issues.

Safety Rules for Contractors

In order that all subcontractors are made aware of BW’s policy with regard to Health & Safety, a copy of our company Health & Safety and environmental handbook is issued with every subcontractor order placed. Prior to commencing their works on site, all subcontractors are required to attend a site induction allowing our project managers to ensure that all operatives are made fully aware of both BW’s and the site’s rules. On completion of the induction, all operatives are required to sign a site rules acceptance Record confirming their agreement to the site rules and their understanding of all issues covered in the HS&E handbook.

Construction and (Design & Management) Regulations 2015

We, as a company, are well versed in the implementation of the construction (design and management) regulations 2015 and are committed to ensure full compliance with the regulations. All parties involved in a project are required to ensure strict enforcement of the construction (design and management) regulations 2015 and associated rules and guidelines.

Health & Safety Advisors

As well as our own Health & Safety manager, we employ the services of SHE Management (UK) Limited in the capacity of Health & Safety advisors, utilising both their health and safety training and their site inspection and reporting services.

We are also members of the British Safety Council; Membership no. 127550 This allows us to make use of their advisory, training and Health & Safety auditing facilities.

Training

We understand the importance of all training. We feel that Health & Safety training is essential and needs to be given appropriate priority in order to maintain an up-to-date knowledge of legal requirements, techniques and procedures. It is therefore important to train our employees for both legal and commercial reasons. For this reason, we follow a prepared Health & Safety training schedule.

We would confirm that all members of our team are trained to the relevant level depending on their responsibilities and job roles. This training includes CSCS, IOSH senior executive and construction managing safely, SMSTS, SSSTS and specialist training on an as required basis (records are available at your request).

We believe that new employees at whatever level need to be inducted at the earliest opportunity. The initial induction is carried out immediately an employee starts. All employees are issued with a copy of the staff handbook, which indicates various systems and procedures that need to be adopted by all employee.
