Project Description
Flexible solutions create a new hub for tech talent on the City fringe.

Amazon chose BW to fit out the award-winning 6th London building near Smithfield Market to help the company significantly expand and attract new talent. 15 new company acquisitions during the build required a flexible approach by the BW team to ensure the changing brief could be successfully met, while maintaining the original programme and budget. Extremely close working relations between all companies and the client’s design team were essential to manage the complex branding strategy. An integrated design approach allowed the smaller firms to maintain their identity and reflect the parent company’s global brand. Through collaboration and a rigorous approach to quality, Amazon now have a vibrant creative and innovative flexible work environment to attract the best tech talent and inspire innovation.

Scope and Services
  • Multiple phase Cat B refurbishments in a busy City location over 11 office floors, plus basement and roof plant.
  • Upgrade of the base build reception area, open plan highly branded workspace, 26 bespoke “flex” rooms, commercial kitchen, restaurant and terrace, breakout areas, copy points and bespoke storage units. Innovative sliding/folding wall systems allow for 24 different configurations. High tech security systems link all the global offices under 1 security system. Supply and install of all furniture and move management.
Challenges
  • Following delays to the base build BW works needed to be carried out in parallel to the main contractors, without impacting programme.
  • Manage the ever changing branding requirements of 15 separate company acquisitions while integrating Amazon’s global brand.
The BW Added Value
  • Shared commitment with the client to overcome the challenges of a changing brief.
  • Safely delivering 100% defect free £3million joinery package together with bespoke lighting schemes for all of the different companies.
